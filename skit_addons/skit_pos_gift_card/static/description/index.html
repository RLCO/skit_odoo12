Gift Card in POS

This application helps you to send Gift to the customer.

Watch the Video

Create Product

First have to create a new product as Gift Card.

Then click on Gift Card button it will show popup to enter the details.

After confirming the details product gets add to the cart.

Check Gift Product

While create a new session,

Select the mandatory "Gift Product" as Gift Card.

Product Added to Cart

Confirming details, product gets added to cart.

Gift Card

After validated the products.

Then click on Gift Card button to download gift card, the details which were entered in the popup.

Backend Process

Go to POS module from that click on Gift Card menu.

After validating the gift product, the details of the gift product sent to the customer to be created in backend.

Gift Card Receipt

Select the line & click on print button it will show selection item from that click on Gift Card Receipt.

Receipt gets downloaded

Redeem Gift Card

Create a new product "Gift Voucher". Then using the generated secret code you can redeem the gift card.
On POS click "Manage Gift Coupon" to redeem gift card. Then give the secret code, after entering the secret code the amount of the gift card will be displayed.

User can use gift card partially.

Can maintain the voucher details in the "Voucher History" Menu.

Check the availability of the voucher amount.
