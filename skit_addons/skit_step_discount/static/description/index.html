Step Discount

Watch the video

Configuration

Goto Sales->Step Discount window. Create new discount you want to apply.

Sales

How does step discount works in Sales module

In Sales order lines, there is a column step discount displayed with list of discount created. select product and step discount you want to apply. Step discount applied before tax apply for product price.

Example product price =750

tax = 15%
step discount=10%

Then ,

10% of product price =(750*5%) =>75

actual_price of product=750-75 = 675

If tax avaiable for product,then

15%tax applied for product = 675*15% = 101.25

Step Discount in POS

Before Proceed to Step discount in POS,Please confirm "Step discount" product and its available in POS.

Select the Product in POS. click on step discount button near by customer button.

New Popup will displayed with list of step discount. select the discount and click OK button

Step discounts will be applied before tax calculation.

Without SD

Actual product price :800.40

Tax 15% ,15% of 800.40 = 120.06

With SD

Step discount:5%

then, Apply discount before tax

5% 800.4o = 40.02

Total: 800.40— 40.02=760.38

Apply 15%tax on 760.38 = 114.06
